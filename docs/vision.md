# Vision

## The Problem

Software engineers spend hours adapting their CVs, preparing applications,
answering repetitive questions and trying to understand why they are rejected.

Most platforms help people find jobs.

Very few help people become stronger candidates.

---

## Our Mission

SkillForge helps software engineers understand what the market demands,
identify skill gaps, and continuously improve their professional profile.

---

## Target Users

- Backend Engineers
- Fullstack Developers
- DevOps Engineers
- AI Engineers
- Students
- Career Changers

---

## Core Principles

- The candidate owns their data.
- AI assists, but does not replace critical thinking.
- Every recommendation should be actionable.
- Learning should be personalized.

---

## What SkillForge Is NOT

- A job board.
- Another LinkedIn.
- A resume builder.
- A chatbot.

SkillForge is a Career Engineering Platform.
